Around mid-2021, during the peak of the COVID-19 delta variant outbreak, the Kawal COVID-19 team were also involved in the development of [wargabantuwarga.com](https://wargabantuwarga.com). WargaBantuWarga is an information resource made by the citizens, for the citizens, providing contact details for health facilities and medical supplies providers, as well as providing a platform for fellow citizens to help each other overcome the COVID-19 pandemic.

[You can view the source code here](https://github.com/kawalcovid19/wargabantuwarga.com).

## Technologies Used

- [React](https://reactjs.org/)
- [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/)
